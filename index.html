Biography

Colin Shanafelt is an accomplished author, world traveler, U.S. Air Force veteran, and Professor of English at The University of Texas at Austin. His diverse experiences span military service, educational innovation, global exploration, and literary achievement.

Professor Shanafelt is a U.S. Air Force veteran who deployed to South America and the Middle East for forward air control ground combat missions, demonstrating exceptional service to his country.

In 2012, after creating a successful suite of educational applications for students of writing, rhetoric, and literature, he embarked on an extraordinary journey with his wife and daughter, traveling around the world and visiting twenty-five countries in fifteen months.

Professor Shanafelt currently teaches in the Department of Rhetoric & Writing at The University of Texas at Austin, where he continues to inspire and educate the next generation of writers and thinkers.
