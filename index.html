Biography

Colin Shanafelt is an author, traveler, veteran, and Professor of English. His published works include What Gods Would Be Theirs? (novel), One Thing Right (children's book), and Literary Analysis & Writing Guide (textbook). His unpublished second novel Apotheosis chronicles the run-up to general artificial intelligence via quantum computing.

Professor Shanafelt is a U.S. Air Force veteran who deployed to South America and the Middle East for forward air control ground combat missions.

In 2012, after creating a successful suite of educational applications for students of writing, rhetoric, and literature, he traveled around the world with his wife and daughter, eventually visiting twenty-five countries in fifteen months.

Professor Shanafelt currently teaches in the Department of Rhetoric & Writing at The University of Texas at Austin.
